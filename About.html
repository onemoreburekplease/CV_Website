Hello!

My name is Amar Zulić, I'm a graphic designer and motion designer from Ljubljana, Slovenia.

I'm currently a 25 year old student finishing my studies.

I have completed my Bachelor's degree in Marketing at the University of Ljubljana, School of Economics, and am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Tourism Marketing.

Technical Info

I have been drawing since childhood. I tend to practice every day, even now. Being a strong hobby of mine, drawing sparked my interest in other forms of visual creativity, so I started teaching myself how to create digitally. I started experimenting with Photoshop about 10 years ago, which I used to genereally create custom gifts for friends and family members. Later on, I started learning other software, eventually moving to animation, 3D design, and motion design. My last couple of jobs also thought me web design, so I've coded this entire website myself, and I really enjoyed the process.

I am extremely interested in everything. I love 3D design as much as I like, for example, coding in Android studio. Some fields of design I realitically know more, such as motion design in After Effects or vector design in Illustrator, but I am always looking for opportunities to learn and explore elsewhere.

Software I use: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe InDesign, Blender3D, Figma, Unity, coding languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), and more.

Projects I have gathered on this website are a mix of personal and client work. Some projects are from my student jobs, some are University projects, and the rest are generally projects made from pure joy.

I should also note that I personally really enjoy brainstorming and researching. I believe studying marketing really enhances some aspects of design, such as understanding market conditions, understanding the target audience, benchmarking the competition, conducting market research, measuring the results, and much more.
