Pozdrav!

My name is Amar Zulić, I am a graphic designer and motion designer based in Ljubljana.

I've completed my bachelor's degree in Marketing and Advertizing at the University of Ljubljana, School of Economics, and am currently pursuing a master's degree in Tourism Marketing.

I find ways to combine marketing with design.

I have worked in many different fields of design, I've done print to digital, 2D to 3D, static to motion, and I am currently exploring Web development and Interactive design. Web design and coding is something that (at least currently) interests me most.

Technical Info

Software I use: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe InDesign, Blender, Figma, coding languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), and more.

Projects I have gathered on this website are a mix of personal and client work. Some projects are from my student jobs, some are University projects, some of them are projects made from pure joy.

Every project usually combines multiple softwares.

I should also note that I personally really enjoy brainstorming and researching. I believe brands can have a successful personality if they research their market well. Every idea should have a goal and a purpose, and I really enjoy finding those.
